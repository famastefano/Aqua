\section{Concept}
Aqua is an 3D Engine targeting realistic simulations in military environments.
It follows the DRY and SOLID principles, component-based design,
and it's my take on how a large and complexe software shall be written.

STL data structures have been rewritten to support my take on how
an allocator shall be used by them, rather being part of a type.
